HMS Library

Founded in 2002

Libraries are major sources of information for society and they serve as guardians of the public's access to information. The advent of the digital world has revolutionized how the public obtains its information and how libraries provide it.



HMS Auditorium

Founded in 2002

An auditorium is also known as an assembly hall. It is an essential part of a school as it has multiple uses to organize celebrations and recreational activities. A majority of schools use their auditorium on a daily basis for their morning prayers and assembly.



HMS Cafeteria

Founded in 2002

A café is a type of restaurant which typically serves coffee and tea, in addition to light refreshments such as baked goods or snacks. The term "café" comes from the French word meaning "coffee".
